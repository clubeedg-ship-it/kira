## 2. Unique Value by Segment

### Healthcare Value Proposition
- **Rehabilitation:** Gamified movement therapy â€” patients engage naturally with projected games, improving adherence to rehab programs (gait, balance, cognitive)
- **Elderly/dementia care:** Reduces agitation, stimulates social interaction, provides non-pharmacological engagement
- **Pediatric wards:** Distraction therapy during procedures, anxiety reduction, makes hospitals less scary
- **Waiting rooms:** Reduces perceived wait times, calms anxious patients/families
- **Hygiene:** Touchless interaction (motion/gesture) = infection control advantage over tablets/touchscreens

### Corporate Value Proposition
- **Lobby "wow factor":** Brand storytelling, visitor engagement, modern image projection
- **Wellness/break areas:** Stress relief through playful interaction, movement encouragement for sedentary workers
- **Meeting rooms:** Interactive presentations, collaborative brainstorming on projected surfaces
- **Talent attraction:** "Cool office" factor for recruiting, especially in creative/tech industries
- **Event spaces:** Adaptable for corporate events, product launches, client experiences

## 5. Strategic Priorities

1. **Healthcare is the highest-margin, most defensible opportunity.** Tovertafel proved Dutch-origin interactive projection can win globally in care. IAM's floor/wall capability is a natural differentiator (whole-body movement vs. tabletop hand gestures). Prioritize this.

2. **Corporate is high-value but bespoke.** Start with 2-3 showcase installations (ideally Dutch tech companies or co-working spaces) to build a portfolio. Partner with AV integrators rather than selling direct.

3. **Children's market is the cash cow.** Don't neglect it â€” use it to fund healthcare/corporate expansion. Tighten the subscription/content model for recurring revenue.

4. **Build a case study library.** Each new segment needs 3-5 reference cases before scaling. Offer pilot pricing to early adopters in exchange for testimonials and data.

5. **Localize the advantage.** IAM is Dutch. Tovertafel is Dutch. The Netherlands has a strong reputation in healthcare innovation. Use "Dutch design + Dutch healthcare expertise" as a credibility anchor internationally.
