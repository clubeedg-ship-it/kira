## 2. IAM Kindergarten Sales Pipeline

### Finding Decision-Makers in Netherlands/Benelux

**The goldmine: Landelijk Register Kinderopvang (LRK)**
- Official Dutch government register of ALL childcare facilities
- URL: https://duo.nl/particulier/landelijk-register-kinderopvang.jsp
- Contains: name, address, type, municipality of every registered kinderopvang
- **~38,000 childcare locations in NL** (kinderdagverblijf, BSO, peuterspeelzaal)
- Public data ‚Äî can be scraped or downloaded

**How to build your list:**
1. Download/scrape LRK for all "kinderdagverblijf" and "peuterspeelzaal" entries
2. Use Apollo.io or LinkedIn to find directors/managers (locatiemanager, directeur)
3. Enrich with email using Apollo/Hunter.io
4. Large chains to target first: KidsFoundation, Partou, Kindergarden, CompaNanny, Smallsteps

**Key decision-makers:**
- **Locatiemanager** (location manager) ‚Äî for individual locations
- **Regiomanager** (regional manager) ‚Äî for chains, controls multiple locations
- **Bestuur/Directie** ‚Äî board/management for independent kindergartens
- **Inkoop/Facility manager** ‚Äî for large organizations

### Best Sales Channels (ranked)

1. **LinkedIn + warm DMs** (üèÜ #1 for NL) ‚Äî Dutch business culture values relationships. Connect ‚Üí value message ‚Üí demo offer
2. **Phone calls** (#2) ‚Äî Kindergartens answer phones. Direct, personal. Call 10:00-11:30 or 14:00-15:30 (outside pickup/dropoff)
3. **Email** (#3) ‚Äî General info@ emails often ignored. Need personal emails.
4. **Trade shows** (#4) ‚Äî Expensive but high-intent:
   - **Kinderopvang Totaal** (annual, NL) ‚Äî THE event for childcare
   - **BETT Show** (London) ‚Äî EdTech, international buyers
   - **Didacta** (Germany) ‚Äî Education trade fair
5. **Partnerships/Distributors** ‚Äî See below

### Do Kindergartens Buy Direct or Through Distributors?

**Both, but distributors dominate for hardware:**
- EyeClick sells through **reseller network** (AV integrators, educational suppliers)
- WizeFloor sells through **country distributors** (e.g., WizeFloor UK is a separate entity)
- Dutch educational suppliers: **Heutink**, **De Rolf Groep**, **Nienhuis Montessori**
- AV integrators who serve education: **BIS Group**, **Crestron partners**

**Recommendation:** Start with **direct sales** to prove the product, then recruit 2-3 distributors once you have 10+ reference installations.

### Message That Converts

**Subject line:** "Interactief spelen op de vloer ‚Äî gratis demo voor [naam locatie]"

**Key angles:**
- Children learn through movement + play (backed by Dutch "spelend leren" philosophy)
- Works for all ages, inclusive for children with special needs
- Low maintenance ‚Äî no toys to clean/replace
- "Wow factor" for parents ‚Üí competitive advantage for enrollment
- Already used by [X] kinderdagverblijven in NL (social proof)

**CTA:** Free 30-minute live demo at your location (bring the projector, let kids play)
