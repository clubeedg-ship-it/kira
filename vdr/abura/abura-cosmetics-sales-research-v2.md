## Executive Summary

Abura Cosmetics is a Netherlands-based natural skincare brand offering organic body and face oils with African and Mediterranean heritage positioning. The brand has certifications (COSMOS Organic, Vegan, PETA Cruelty-Free) and retail presence at two Amsterdam boutiques (Dany Diop stores).

**Honest Assessment:**
- Currently D2C-focused with **no visible wholesale infrastructure**
- Products appear **perpetually discounted** (50% off) — true pricing unclear
- **Product sizes (100ml) not optimized for professional B2B use** (spas need 250ml+)
- B2B expansion is viable but requires **12+ months, not 6 months**
- Execution requires dedicated resources (minimum 15-20 hrs/week or hire)

**This strategy prioritizes:**
1. Quick wins (independent spas, indie retailers, PR coverage)
2. Building proof points before approaching major accounts
3. Realistic timelines based on industry norms
4. Budget-conscious execution

## Pricing Clarification ⚠️

**Current website shows:**

| Product | "Regular Price" | Sale Price | Discount |
|---------|-----------------|------------|----------|
| Dry Body Oil (100ml) | €49.95 | €24.95 | 50% |
| Nautilus Face Oil (30ml) | €49.00 | €34.95 | 29% |
| AquaRadiance Face Mist (50ml) | €19.00 | €14.95 | 21% |
| Bronze Body Glow (30ml) | €24.95 | €19.95 | 20% |

**Recommendation:** If products have been at "sale" prices for 60+ days, the sale price IS the real price. EU consumer law (Omnibus Directive 2019/2161) requires displaying the lowest price from the past 30 days. Perpetual sales can trigger regulatory issues and undermine brand credibility.

**For strategy purposes, we'll use current selling prices (€15-€35 range).**

## Regulatory & Compliance Notes

| Requirement | Status | Action Needed |
|-------------|--------|---------------|
| CPNP (Cosmetic Products Notification Portal) | Likely complete (legally required to sell in EU) | Verify with founder |

### UK (Post-Brexit) — If Targeting

| Requirement | Complexity | Cost Estimate |
|-------------|------------|---------------|
| SCPN (UK Cosmetic Products Notification) | Medium | £500-2,000 per product |
| UK Responsible Person | Required | £1,000-3,000/year (outsourced) |
| Customs declarations | Ongoing | Per shipment |
| UK VAT registration | Required if >£85K revenue | Free to register |

**Recommendation:** UK market entry costs £5,000-15,000 minimum. Defer until EU is profitable.

### USA — If Targeting

| Requirement | Complexity | Cost Estimate |
|-------------|------------|---------------|

| State-level regulations (California Prop 65, etc.) | High | Testing: $1,000-3,000 |
| US fulfillment setup | High | $5,000-20,000 startup |
| Product liability insurance (US) | Required | $2,000-5,000/year |

**Recommendation:** US market requires $20,000-50,000 minimum investment. Not viable in Year 1.

## Sales Channels: Realistic Prioritization

### TIER 1: Quick Wins (Months 1-6)

These channels have shorter sales cycles and lower barriers to entry.

#### 1. Independent Day Spas (Retail Only)

**Why:** Owners make buying decisions quickly. No corporate procurement.

**Target profile:**
- Single-location or 2-3 location spas
- Already carrying indie/boutique products
- Focus on Netherlands + Belgium first (no customs)

**Realistic targets:**
- 20-30 prospecting emails/week
- Expect 10-15% response rate (2-4 conversations)
- Close rate: 20-30% of conversations
- **Goal: 3-5 spa retail accounts in 6 months**

**Cost:**
| Item | Cost |
|------|------|
| Discovery Set samples (20) | €500 |
| Shipping | €100 |
| Time: 5-10 hrs/week | €0 or hire |
| Total | ~€600 + time |

#### 2. Indie Beauty Retailers (Local First)

**Why:** Independent stores make faster decisions than chains.

**Target profile:**
- Concept stores, wellness boutiques, organic shops
- Netherlands, Belgium, Germany
- Stores already carrying niche/indie beauty

**NOT targeting yet:** Space NK, Cult Beauty, Credo — these require 6-12 month buyer cycles, proven sales data, and often exclusivity. Save for Year 2.

**Realistic targets:**
- Research 50 indie stores

- **Goal: 2-3 indie retail accounts in 6 months**

**Cost:**

#### 3. Beauty PR & Press Coverage

**Why:** Editorial coverage builds credibility that helps ALL other channels. One Vogue mention > 50 influencer posts.

**Target outlets:**
- **NL:** ELLE Netherlands, Grazia, LINDA, Glamour NL
- **EU:** Vogue (online), Refinery29, The Zoe Report, Into The Gloss
- **Niche:** Clean beauty blogs, wellness publications

**Approach:**
- Create press kit (brand story, founder story, high-res images, product samples)
- Send targeted pitches to beauty editors (not mass blast)
- Timing: Align with seasonal themes (summer glow, winter hydration, Valentine's gifts)

**Realistic outcome:**
- **Goal: 3-5 press mentions in 6 months** (mix of big and small outlets)

**Cost:**
| Item | Cost |
|------|------|
| Press kit creation | €200-500 (design) |
| Product samples for press | €300-500 |
| Optional: PR freelancer | €500-2,000/month |
| DIY Total | ~€500-1,000 |
| With freelancer | ~€3,500-7,000 for 3 months |

### TIER 2: Medium-Term Channels (Months 6-12)

These require more setup, longer sales cycles, or proof points from Tier 1.

#### 6. Influencer Partnerships (Strategic, Not Spray-and-Pray)

**Why:** Social proof matters, but ROI varies wildly.

**Approach:**
- Focus on **micro-influencers** (5K-30K followers) — better engagement, lower cost
- Prioritize content creators who match brand values (wellness, sustainability, melanin-inclusive)
- Affiliate model (commission-based) over paid posts

**Do NOT:**
- Gift 50 random influencers and hope for posts
- Pay for sponsored posts without performance tracking

**Realistic outcome:**
- **Goal: 10-15 influencer partnerships with affiliate codes**
- **Expected posts: 15-25 over 6 months**

**Cost:**
| Item | Cost |
|------|------|
| Product gifting (15 influencers) | €375 |
| Shipping | €75 |
| Affiliate commission (10-15%) | Variable |
| Optional: Influencer platform subscription | €100-300/month |
| Total (conservative) | ~€500-1,500 |

---

#### 7. Wholesale Platforms (Faire, etc.)

**Why:** Passive inbound from retailers browsing platforms.

**Platforms:**
- **Faire** — Best for indie retailers (EU + US)
- **Creoate** — European B2B marketplace
- **Ankorstore** — Strong in France/Southern Europe

**Requirements:**
- Professional product photos
- Wholesale pricing (typically 50% of retail)
- Minimum order quantities (MOQ)
- Clear shipping terms

**Realistic outcome:**
- **Goal: 5-10 inbound inquiries in first 6 months on platform**
- **Convert: 2-3 orders**

**Cost:**
| Item | Cost |
|------|------|
| Platform setup | €0 (commission-based) |
| Photography upgrade | €300-800 |
| Time: 2-3 hrs/week managing | €0 or hire |
| Platform commission | 15-25% of wholesale |
| Total startup | ~€300-800 |

#### 8. Professional Distributors (Research Phase)

**Why:** Distributors have existing spa/salon relationships. They sell for you.

**EU Distributors to research:**
- Mayr Beauty (Netherlands)
- Beauty & More (Germany)
- Naos International
- Biologique Recherche network

**What they want:**

- Exclusivity (often by region)

**In Months 6-12:** Research and identify 3-5 potential distributors. Approach with sales data from Tier 1 wins.

**Cost:** Research time only. Actual partnership discussions in Year 2.

### TIER 3: Long-Term Channels (Year 2+)

Not realistic in first 12 months without significant investment.

#### Deferred: Major Retailers (Space NK, Cult Beauty, Sephora)

**Reality check:**
- 6-12 month buyer cycles minimum
- Often require proven velocity data from smaller retailers
- May require margin concessions or exclusivity
- Marketing commitments (often €10K+ for launch support)

**Action in Year 1:** Build relationships, attend their open calls/brand days, get on their radar. Don't expect placement.

#### Deferred: Trade Shows (Cosmoprof)

**Reality check:**
- Booth costs: €5,000-25,000
- Travel, accommodation, samples: €2,000-5,000
- Best ROI when you have a solid offering to show

**Action in Year 1:** Attend as visitor (€200-500), take meetings, learn. Exhibit in Year 2.

#### Deferred: US Market Entry

**Reality check:**
- $20,000-50,000+ minimum investment
- Regulatory, logistics, marketing all require significant setup

**Action in Year 1:** None. Revisit when EU is profitable and producing 6-figure revenue.
