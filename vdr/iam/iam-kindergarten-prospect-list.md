# IAM Interactive Move — Kindergarten Prospect List (Netherlands)
*Compiled: February 2026 | Source: LRK, Kinderopvang-Wijzer Top 100, company websites*

## Targeting Strategy

**Priority order:** Large chains first (one deal = many locations), then progressive/premium independents, then regional players.

**Decision-maker titles to target:**
- Directeur/Bestuurder (Director/Board)
- Regiomanager (Regional Manager)
- Manager Huisvesting & Faciliteiten (Facilities Manager)
- Hoofd Pedagogiek (Head of Pedagogy)
- Inkoop/Procurement
