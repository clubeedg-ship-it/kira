## A/B Testing Notes

- Test subject lines in batches of 50-100
- Track: open rate (subject line effectiveness) and reply rate (body effectiveness)
- Dutch market responds well to: direct, no-nonsense, slightly informal ("Hoi" vs "Geachte")
- For chains/corporates: use "Beste" (more formal)
- For independents: "Hoi" works fine
- Always include opt-out: "Niet meer mailen? Laat het me weten."
