# Team Framework Per Company
*How to structure human + AI teams for each Oopuo company*

## Overview

Each company operates with a hybrid team model:
- **AI Core:** Kira + specialized sub-agents
- **Human Leadership:** Domain experts, decision-makers
- **Execution:** Mix of AI automation and human work

---

## Company-Specific Frameworks

### ZenithCred (FinTech)

**Team Structure:**
```
Otto (CEO) ← Strategic decisions
    │
Kira (COO) ← Operations coordination
    │
    ├── Credit Agent ← Risk assessment, scoring
    ├── Compliance Agent ← Regulatory monitoring
    └── Customer Agent ← Support, onboarding
    
Human Needs:
- Compliance Officer (required by law)
- Senior Credit Analyst (model oversight)
- Customer Success Lead (high-touch accounts)
```

**Key Handoffs:**
- AI → Human: Credit decisions above threshold, regulatory filings
- Human → AI: Data processing, routine assessments, reports

## Cross-Company Communication

### Async (Default)
- Notion pages for projects
- @chimera_activity_bot for real-time updates
- Email for formal communications

### Sync (Scheduled)
- Weekly company check-ins (15 min each)
- Monthly all-hands (virtual)
- Quarterly strategy sessions

### Escalation
| Level | Path | SLA |
|-------|------|-----|
| Routine | Notion comment | 24h |
| Urgent | Telegram DM | 4h |
| Critical | Phone call | 1h |
