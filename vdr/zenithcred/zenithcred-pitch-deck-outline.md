## 2. Solution (What We Build)

### ZenithCred: "Light Up Your Workforce"
Physical wellness infrastructure that makes office spaces active participants in employee health.

### Core Components
1. **Interactive Light Panels**: Responsive ambient lighting that changes based on team wellness metrics
2. **Gamified Team Challenges**: Points, rewards, and social competition for movement, mindfulness, hydration
3. **Biofeedback Integration**: Connects with Oura, WHOOP, Apple Health for real-time wellness data
4. **Environmental Response**: Office lights literally "glow healthier" as team wellness improves
5. **Privacy-First Design**: Team metrics shown, individual data remains private (GDPR compliant)

### Unique Value Proposition
- **Hardware + Software**: Unlike pure software competitors, we create persistent physical presence
- **Team-Based**: Social gamification drives sustained engagement vs. individual apps
- **Ambient Intelligence**: Wellness becomes part of the environment, not another screen to check
- **Real-Time Feedback**: Light panels provide instant visual celebration of wellness achievements

### Light Therapy Science Foundation
- Clinical evidence for mood improvement, circadian regulation, stress reduction
- 10,000 lux therapeutic lighting integrated into gamification system
- Morning light exposure for energy, evening warm tones for relaxation
