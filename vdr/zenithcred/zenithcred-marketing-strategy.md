### Key Stats to Highlight

**Must Have:**
- Number of paying customers
- Primary engagement/outcome metric (sick day reduction, daily engagement %)
- ARR or MRR
- Target raise amount
- Market size (focus on addressable, not TAM fluff)

**Nice to Have:**
- Customer logos (if permitted)
- NPS or testimonial quote
- Team backgrounds (logos of previous employers)
- Notable advisors or angels committed

### Design Tips
- Use brand colors consistently
- Include 1-2 product images (light panels in action)
- Keep text scannable (bullets, not paragraphs)
- No more than 300 words total
- PDF format, <2MB
- Name file: `ZenithCred-OnePager-[Month][Year].pdf`

### Call-to-Action Options
- "Let's schedule 20 minutes: [Calendly link]"
- "Reply to this email for our full deck"
- "Interested? otto@zenithcred.com"
