## Outreach Strategy Notes

1. **Lead with the hardware moat** — most VCs see endless SaaS wellness pitches. The physical product is the differentiator.
2. **IAM traction is your proof point** — 50+ kindergarten deployments proves the hardware works at scale.
3. **Target Dutch funds first** — home market, easier meetings, faster due diligence.
4. **Get warm intros** — cold emails convert at ~2% for seed rounds. Use Oopuo network, IAM customers, and Dutch startup ecosystem (StartupDelta, TechLeap) for introductions.
5. **Apply to accelerators as backup:** YES!Delft (NL), Startupbootcamp Health (NL), Flying Health (DE) can provide funding + investor access.
