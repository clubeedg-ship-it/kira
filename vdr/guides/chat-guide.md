## How It Works

Kira's chat isn't a search bar. It's a persistent conversation with an AI that knows your context, remembers your history, and can take real actions.

Messages sync in real-time. When Kira is working on something complex, you'll see her thought process unfold live.

## What You Can Do

### Ask Questions
Kira has access to your documents, knowledge graph, and conversation history. Ask naturally:

> "What do we know about Project X?"
> "Summarize the quarterly report"
> "Who are the key contacts at Company Y?"

### Manage Work
Create and track tasks through conversation:

> "Create a task to review the proposal by Friday"
> "What's overdue?"
> "Mark the design review as done"

### Analyze & Research
Kira can dive deep:

> "Compare our pricing to competitors"
> "What are the risks in this approach?"
> "Draft an outline for the investor deck"

### Generate Content
Documents, reports, emails, strategies:

> "Write a project brief for the new feature"
> "Draft a follow-up email to the client"
> "Create a competitive analysis"

---

## Tips for Better Results

1. **Be specific.** "Analyze Q1 revenue by segment" beats "tell me about revenue."

2. **Give context.** "Based on yesterday's meeting, draft action items" helps Kira connect the dots.

3. **Ask follow-ups.** Kira maintains context within a conversation. Build on previous messages.

4. **Use her memory.** Kira remembers past conversations. Reference them: "Remember when we discussed the pricing model?"

5. **Let her show her work.** If an answer seems off, expand the thinking block to see her reasoning. You can correct course.
