## What We're Building

### Chimera Protocol

*Full technical vision: See `CHIMERA-PROTOCOL.md`*

**One sentence:** Unkillable, privacy-preserving AI compute that exists because people use it.

**The Core:**
- **Split-Brain** - Consultant (local, private) creates blind Job Tickets → Savant (distributed, stateless) executes without knowing who asked
- **ZK Membrane** - Cryptographic proof of right-to-access without revealing identity
- **Distributed** - No central server. No Chimera HQ. Nodes contribute compute for tokens.
- **Unkillable** - Usage = uptime. Can't be turned off because there's nothing to turn off.

**What This Means:**
Not an AI product. Not a company with servers. Infrastructure that spreads like a virus and exists wherever there's a willing host. Privacy by architecture, not by promise.

**The goal**: Define it. Build it. Ship it. Prove this model works. Stay independent. Can't be bought, can't be shut down.
