# LinkedIn Workspace — Otto / Oopuo

## Structure
- `strategy/` — Overall LinkedIn strategy, posting schedule, growth plan
- `content/` — Ready-to-post content (numbered by week/day)
- `templates/` — Connection request templates, DM templates

## Companies Covered
- **Personal (Otto)** — Founder brand, ties everything together
- **InterActiveMove (IAM)** — Interactive projection systems
- **ZenithCred** — Corporate wellness gamification
- **OttoGen** — AI services & content

## Posting Schedule
- **Tuesday 8:00 CET** — Main post (carousel or long-form)
- **Wednesday 8:00 CET** — Engagement post (question, poll, insight)
- **Thursday 8:00 CET** — Company spotlight or case study

## Daily Routine (15-20 min)
1. Post the scheduled content (copy-paste from content/)
2. Send 20 connection requests (use templates/)
3. Comment on 5 posts from target connections
