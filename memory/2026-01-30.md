## Chimera Vision (Crystallized Today)

**Split-Brain Architecture:**
- Consultant (local) - Has your data, creates anonymized Job Tickets
- Savant (distributed) - Blind executors, stateless, never know who asked
- ZK Membrane - Cryptographic proof of right-to-access without identity

**Key Properties:**
- Unkillable - No central point. Usage = uptime.
- Spreads like a virus - Wherever there's a willing host
- Self-paying - Nodes contribute compute for tokens

**Otto's words:** "Not a Chimera server at Chimera building but something that spreads like a virus in the whole network."

**Architecture simplified:**
```
[User] ‚Üê‚Üí [Local Consultant] ‚Üê‚Üí [Job Queue] ‚Üê‚Üí [Distributed Savants]
         (realtime, private)     (async)        (public, blind, cheap)
```

Local handles the relationship. Distributed is just labor. Privacy stays local.
